Max gets really reactive when he sees other dogs on walks. We've been doing the calm anchoring but he still lunges sometimes. Any advice?
🤖
Leona
That's actually really common with German Shepherds, Marisol! Max is showing classic Structured Guardian behavior — protective but overwhelmed. Here's what I recommend:
1. Start the calm anchor 30 seconds BEFORE you see the trigger
2. Use the "3-2-1 Breath" technique from Module 2
3. Increase distance — don't force proximity yet
